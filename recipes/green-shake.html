Green Shake

Description

This is a favorite of mine to whip up after having the kids toil in the fields of my 50 acre homestead. The children despise the flavor, which is how I know I got the blend just right. It is so cute to see them blech and retch as they force down the drink knowing if they don't finish, there will be no other food that day. The cherry on top is the meek little thank you they give that I know is insincere, but I rather have politeness and respect than honesty.

I learned to like this recipe growing up. My mom and her mom and generations before used this recipe. Sometimes I wake up in the middle of the night sweating, rubbing my head, and remembering how my mother would smack me with a wooden spoon if I didn't finish the drink. Then I am awashed with an uncontrollable urge to cry, so I pinch myself hard, sometimes hard enough to bleed, to distract myself from the turmoil and agony roiling inside. I am thankful she taught me the meaning of respect.

Here's the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Mango

  • Pineapple

  • Spinach

  • Romain Lettuce

  • Soy Milk

Steps

  1. Use a blender

  2. Add liquid ingredients

  3. Add frozen greens

  4. Add frozen fruit

  5. Blend!

  6. Enjoy
